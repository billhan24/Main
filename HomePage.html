Chinese History

Chinese history influences their way of living. It continues to build off of their own history which, in turn, affects many aspects of their life and how they go through their everyday lives. Chinese history is one of the oldest written histories ever.

Chinese Celebrations

Chinese celebrations are a direct reflection of their morals and values. What chinese people celebrate shows the types of things that they value on a regular basis. For example, dragons, the colour red, and lots of food are things that they believe help to drive Chinese soceity.
Chinese culture has been around for almost 3000 years. It shapes the way Chinese people express their emotions and communication with each other. The purpose of this website is to educate and teach you about China's culture and how it impacts their culture, communication, mannerisms, and traditional values. Chinese culture has so many unique and interesting aspects to it, I encourage you to learn more.

Chinese culture has affected the way Chinese people talk since the beginning of China's history. The way that they communicate is unique and helps them to convey emotion in their own way. Without Chinese culture, a lot of information would get lost in translation with how they feel.
